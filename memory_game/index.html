Instructions

Improve your brain power with this fun and simple game

Concentration, also known as Match Match, Memory, Pelmanism, Shinkei-suijaku, Pexeso or simply Pairs, is a card game in which all of the cars are laid face down on a surface and tow cars are flipped face up over each turn. The object of the game is to turn over pairs of matching cards.
